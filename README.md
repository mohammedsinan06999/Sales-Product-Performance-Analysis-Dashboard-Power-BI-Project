# Sales & Product Performance Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of sales performance and product-level insights using Power BI. The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders monitor sales targets, evaluate team performance, and understand product contribution.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze sales performance against targets
- Evaluate month-over-month (MoM) trends
- Identify top and low-performing salespersons
- Understand product-wise sales distribution
- Provide actionable business insights

---

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Sales Performance
This page focuses on overall sales performance and target analysis.

**Key Metrics:**
- Total Sales: 978.20K  
- Total Target: 4.00M  
- Target Achievement: 24.45%  
- Total Variance: -3.02M  
- MoM Growth: -15.30%  

**Insights:**
- Significant gap between actual sales and targets  
- Decline in sales from February to March  
- Uneven performance across sales team  
- No salesperson achieved full target  

---

### 2. Product Performance
This page focuses on quantity-based product analysis.

**Key Metrics:**
- Total Quantity: 5.49K  
- Total BOX Quantity: 4.42K  
- Total PCS Quantity: 252  
- Best-Selling Product: Egg  

**Insights:**
- Sales are dominated by bulk (BOX) quantities  
- One product contributes significantly to total volume  
- Product demand is not evenly distributed  
- Sales performance varies across salespersons  

---

## Key Insights
- Overall performance is below expectations with only 24.45% target achievement  
- Negative MoM growth indicates declining sales trend  
- High dependency on a single top-performing salesperson  
- Product concentration risk due to reliance on one major product  
- Sales are primarily driven by bulk transactions  

---

## Business Recommendations
- Re-evaluate and align sales targets with realistic performance  
- Improve sales strategy to address declining trends  
- Provide training and support to low-performing salespersons  
- Encourage diversification of product sales  
- Implement continuous performance monitoring
